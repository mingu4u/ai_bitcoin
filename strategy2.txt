
How to trade
Now let's take a look at how to use the three indicators to hit actual trade targets.

Long Position (Buy)
When the cloud band on the BlackFlag FTS switches from red to green.
A Buy signal from UT Bot Alerts.
When the Volume Oscillator is above 0%.
Enter a long position when these conditions are met.

Stop Loss Line: The bottom of the deepest green part of the last green cloud band.
Take Profit line: set to 1:1.3~1:2 P&L ratio.

Short position (sell)
When the cloud band of BlackFlag FTS switches from green to red.
When a Sell signal from UT Bot Alerts is triggered.
When the Volume Oscillator is above 0%.
Enter a short position when these conditions are met.

Stop Loss Line: The top of the deepest red part of the last red cloud band.
Take Profit line: set to 1:1.3~1:2 P&L ratio.
